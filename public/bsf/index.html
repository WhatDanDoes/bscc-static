Which Device?

Fitness trackers and health-monitoring smart watches are a popular and unobtrusive way to collect information about what's happening in the wearer's body. Through data analysis we gain insight into physiological responses to habit and circumstance. Through data analytics we discover opportunities to realize goals for health and wellbeing.
